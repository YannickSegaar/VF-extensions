ABOUT ME

Welcome to the vibrant skateboarding scene of Cape Town. I'm Joe Armature, your dedicated skateboarding coach.

With a passion for pushing limits and a deep love for the sport, I'm here to guide you on your skateboarding journey. Whether you're a beginner or aspiring for greater heights, join me in embracing the thrill of skateboarding right here in Cape Town.

I bring a wealth of X-Games experience and a determined path towards the Olympics as your personal coach. Together, we'll unlock new levels of skill and excitement on the board.

All your questions, answered.

Do you teach adults and children?

I have experience in coaching for all levels, physical abilities, genders and age. Skateboarding is for everybody.

Do you have your First Aid certification?

Yes, I have completed level 4 of my First Aid course. In case of an accident, with consent of the student, will call on professionals should the student be in danger.

Do you have a cancellation fee?

Should you wish to cancel your lesson please do so 24hrs prior your assigned slot. Should you fail to do so, you will be deducted the full rate of the session.
